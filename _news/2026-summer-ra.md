We are recruiting interns for Summer 2026. The details of the project are written in [this document](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1K1Fs4telcCPcOnFvmPwvx2jJdq5hEutLUMv1l1rDIV8/edit?usp=sharing).
The deadline for applications has been extended from May 4th (Mon) to **11:59PM KST on May 8th (Fri)**.
In order to apply, please fill out the following form (in English): [Application Form](https://forms.gle/BQMvP7krcKfhpso9A).

Please note that all lab business is currently handled in English, so applicants should be prepared to communicate in English for all research-related activities.
Unfortunately, this semester, we can recruit only students from institutions within Korea. Please make sure that you are enrolled in a university or institution in Korea during the internship period.
Application review will begin immediately once the deadline has passed, and candidates who will be interviewed will be contacted within the following week.
