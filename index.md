# Collaborative Social Technologies Lab (CSTL)
> **<i>In an AI-driven world, how do we build better connections?</i>**

The **Collaborative Social Technologies Lab (CSTL)** at KAIST's School of Computing is dedicated to answering this question. Building from our work in online trust and safety, we chart the course for the future of social computing.
