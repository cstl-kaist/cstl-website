Call for Applications – Undergraduate Research Assistants, Winter 2026

We are recruiting interns for Winter 2026. The details of the project is written in this document. The deadline for applications is 11:59PM KST on Nov 3rd (Mon).
In order to apply, please fill out the Application Form (in English).
The compensation for this position is 800,000 KRW per month for a total of two months.
Please note that all lab business is currently handled in English, so applicants should be prepared to communicate in English for all research-related activities.
Application review will begin immediately once the deadline has passed, and candidates who will be interviewed will be contacted within the following week.
