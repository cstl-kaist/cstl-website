Call for Applications – Undergraduate Research Assistants, Summer 2026

We are recruiting interns for Summer 2026. The details of the project is written in this document. The deadline for applications is 11:59PM KST on May 4th (Mon). In order to apply, please fill out theApplication Form (in English). Please note that all lab business is currently handled in English, so applicants should be prepared to communicate in English for all research-related activities. Unfortunately, this semester, we can recruit only students from institutions within Korea. Please make sure that you are enrolled in a university or institution in Korea during the internship period. Application review will begin immediately once the deadline has passed, and candidates who will be interviewed will be contacted within the following week.
